# AI History - Video Requirement Document

## Project Overview

A ~2-minute short video introducing the history of artificial intelligence development, from the Turing Test to the GPT era.

## Content Requirements

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Aspect Ratio | 9:16 (1080x1920) |
| Visual Style | Cartoon illustration |
| Narration Language | English |
| Subtitle Language | English |
| Target Duration | ~120 seconds |
| Target Platform | TikTok / Instagram Reels / Shorts |

## Production Mode

**Cost-first** - Use static cartoon illustrations (via `nanobanana-image`) instead of video assets.

## Content Scope

Standard timeline covering major AI milestones:

1. **1950s** - Alan Turing & The Turing Test
2. **1960s-70s** - Early AI & Expert Systems
3. **1980s-90s** - AI Winter & Resurgence / Deep Blue
4. **2000s-10s** - Machine Learning & Deep Learning Breakthrough
5. **2010s-20s** - Transformers & The GPT Era
6. **Present & Future** - AI Everywhere

## Script Segments (12 narration clips)

1. "The story of artificial intelligence begins in 1950, when Alan Turing asked a groundbreaking question."
2. "Can machines think? Turing proposed the Imitation Game, now known as the Turing Test."
3. "In the 1960s and 70s, researchers built expert systems, programs that mimicked human decision-making."
4. "But early AI was limited by hardware and data. Progress stalled, leading to the first AI Winter."
5. "In 1997, IBM's Deep Blue defeated world chess champion Garry Kasparov, marking a historic comeback."
6. "The 2000s brought a revolution in machine learning, powered by bigger data and faster computers."
7. "In 2012, deep learning made a breakthrough when AlexNet crushed the image recognition competition."
8. "Then in 2017, researchers at Google introduced the Transformer architecture, changing everything."
9. "Transformers enabled GPT models to generate human-like text, images, and even code."
10. "ChatGPT, launched in 2022, brought AI into everyday life for millions of people."
11. "Today, AI is everywhere, from self-driving cars to medical diagnosis and creative arts."
12. "The journey of AI has only just begun. The future is being written, one algorithm at a time."
